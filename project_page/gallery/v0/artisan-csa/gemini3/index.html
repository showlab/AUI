Common Questions

What happens if I'm on vacation?

You can pause your share for up to 2 weeks per season. Just let us know 48 hours in advance.

Is everything certified organic?

We use organic practices but are not certified. We welcome you to visit the farm and see for yourself!

Do you deliver to apartments?

Yes, provided there is a safe place to leave the box or a doorman to receive it.
