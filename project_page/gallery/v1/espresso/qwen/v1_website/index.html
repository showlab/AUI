Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

What happens after my 6-month trial?

After your 6-month trial, you can choose to continue your membership at the regular rate or cancel your subscription. We'll send you a reminder email before your trial ends.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 2 months per year. Please contact our support team to arrange a pause.

What are the maintenance responsibilities?

Members are responsible for basic cleaning and maintenance of equipment. We provide detailed care instructions and can arrange professional cleaning if needed.

How do I return equipment?

We provide return shipping labels for all equipment. Simply package your items securely and ship them back to us using the provided label. We'll inspect the equipment upon receipt.
