FAQ

What’s included for $200/month?+
Pro espresso machine + grinder rental, install and calibration, weekly coaching (6‑month track), maintenance, and support. No deposit.
Do I need to buy beans?+
Yes, beans are separate so you can explore. We recommend 2–3 roasters and provide dial‑in guidance for each roast.
Can I pause or travel?+
We offer a one‑time pause up to 3 weeks with 7‑day notice. Coaching sessions can be stacked before/after travel.
What happens after 6 months?+
Most members either extend month‑to‑month or buy their own setup with our help. We’ll share a personalized gear recommendation based on your preferences.
Cancellation policy+
You can cancel Flex anytime with 10‑day notice; 6‑month members can cancel at the end of the term or switch to Flex.
Maintenance responsibilities+
We handle scheduled maintenance; you’ll perform daily wipe-down and backflush per coach guidance. We provide clear checklists.
