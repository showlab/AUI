10-day Battery

Efficient chipset and adaptive refresh for power that lasts. 60-minute fast charge to 80%.

Real-world endurance varies by usage. Continuous heart-rate monitoring and AOD will impact longevity. Our new adaptive scheduler optimizes background tasks to maximize uptime without compromising responsiveness.

Health Suite

Heart rate, SpO₂, skin temp, and sleep stages with on-device insights.

NovaBand is not a medical device. Metrics are intended for general wellness. Sleep staging uses multi-sensor fusion to estimate REM, light, and deep sleep durations. Historical trends are viewable offline.

50m Water Resistant

Swim-proof with auto workout detection and lap tracking.

Rated 5 ATM under ISO 22810:2010. Suitable for swimming in pools and shallow waters. Not suitable for scuba, high-velocity water, or hot showers/saunas.

Always-On Display

1.9" AMOLED, sapphire glass, 2000 nits peak brightness.

AOD refresh intelligently scales down to conserve power while keeping time and key complications visible. Sapphire glass adds scratch resistance for daily wear.

Multi‑band GPS

Dual-frequency GNSS for accurate routes under dense canopies.

Supports L1/L5 where available for better lock in urban and wooded environments. Offline route breadcrumbs help you find your way back without a phone.

NFC Pay

Leave the wallet. Tap to pay with secure on‑device tokenization.

Payment provisioning is supported in select regions. A PIN can be required every time or after removal detection for added security.

Compatibility

Works with iOS and Android. Bluetooth 5.3 for reliable pairing.

Requires iOS 15+ or Android 10+. Companion app enables deeper insights, firmware updates, and downloadable watch faces.
