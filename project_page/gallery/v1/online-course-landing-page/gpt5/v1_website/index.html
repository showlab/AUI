Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?
Click any Enroll button on a course card or in the header/footer. The Enrollment form will appear with the selected course prefilled.
Can I switch courses after enrolling?
Yes. Reply to your confirmation email or use the Contact section. We’ll update your enrollment within one business day.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. See our Terms of Service for details.
What are the schedules like?
Choose between Flexible pace and Full-time. Full-time courses include more live sessions. Both include asynchronous learning.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes. Upon successful completion, a shareable certificate is issued.
