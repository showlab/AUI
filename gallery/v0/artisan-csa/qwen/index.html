Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a membership? ▼

Signing up is easy! Simply visit our plans page, select your preferred plan, and enter your delivery information. You'll receive a confirmation email with details about your first delivery.

What happens if I miss a delivery? ▼

If you're unable to collect your delivery, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. We can arrange for a rescheduled delivery or provide a refund for that week.

Can I customize my box? ▼

While we offer standard seasonal boxes, we do allow for some customization. Please contact us to discuss options for specific dietary needs or preferences.

Do you deliver to all areas? ▼

We currently serve the greater metropolitan area. Check our delivery map to see if your zip code is within our service area. If not, we may have pickup locations available near you.

What if I have food allergies? ▼

We clearly indicate common allergens in each season's produce. All our products are grown organically without pesticides. For specific concerns, please contact us directly before signing up.
